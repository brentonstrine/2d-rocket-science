Controls

Viewport

  • Use ← ↑ ↓ → keys to pan.
  • Use shift + and shift - to zoom in and out.
  • Scroll up and down to pan vertically, left and right to pan horizontally.
  • Hold shift while scrolling to zoom.

Ship

  • Tap a and d keys to turn port and starbord.

Universe

  • Tap the < and > keys to speed or slow time.
